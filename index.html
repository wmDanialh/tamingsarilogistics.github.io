About

More About Taming Sari Sdn. Bhd.

Taming Sari Engineering & Services provides a comprehensive range of services encompassing engineering planning and administration through its subsidiary, Taming Sari Logistics. Established in 2013, our company boasts over 8 years of experience in delivering exceptional services to esteemed organizations such as MISC, Tiong Nam Heavy Lift, Asturi Metal Builders (M) Sdn Bhd, Kuantan Port Authority, Impact Construction & Engineering Sdn. Bhd., and more.

Over the years, we have cultivated a strong reputation for excellence in logistics, sea fastening, choking, pre-setting onboard, lashing, as well as mechanical and engineering projects. Our expertise extends primarily to local ports in Malaysia, where we have consistently provided top-tier services coupled with the deployment of highly skilled manpower.

At Taming Sari Engineering & Services, our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction remains unwavering. We continue to innovate and adapt to meet the evolving needs of our clients, ensuring seamless and efficient project delivery while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and safety.
